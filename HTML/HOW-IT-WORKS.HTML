How to Use AgriDirect

Your complete guide to connecting farmers with markets. Learn how to make the most of our platform.

For Farmers

Sell your produce directly to vendors, get fair prices, and receive quick payments

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up as a farmer and complete your profile with farm details, location, and verification documents.

2. List Your Products

Add your produce with photos, quantity, quality grade, and pricing. Include harvest dates and certifications.

3. Receive Orders

Get notified when vendors show interest in your products. Review orders and confirm availability.

4. Arrange Delivery

Coordinate pickup with integrated logistics partners or arrange your own delivery method.

5. Get Paid Quickly

Receive secure payments within 24 hours of delivery confirmation directly to your bank account.

Market Insights

Access real-time market prices, demand trends, and forecasts to price your produce competitively.

Build Reputation

Earn ratings and reviews from vendors to establish trust and attract more buyers.

Direct Communication

Chat directly with vendors to negotiate terms, answer questions, and build relationships.

For Vendors

Source quality produce directly from farmers, negotiate prices, and ensure reliable supply

1. Register Your Business

Sign up as a vendor (retailer, wholesaler, exporter, or processor) and complete your business profile.

2. Browse Products

Search and filter through thousands of fresh produce listings. View quality grades, prices, and farmer ratings.

3. Connect with Farmers

Contact farmers directly to discuss requirements, negotiate terms, and verify quality standards.

4. Place Orders

Create purchase orders with specified quantities, delivery schedules, and payment terms.

5. Secure Transactions

Make secure payments through our platform with delivery tracking and quality assurance guarantees.

Price Comparison

Compare prices across multiple farmers to get the best deals on quality produce.

Quality Assurance

View farmer ratings, certifications, and product quality grades before purchasing.

Logistics Support

Track deliveries in real-time and manage your supply chain efficiently.

Key Benefits of Using AgriDirect

Fair & Transparent Pricing

Real-time market data ensures farmers get 20-30% higher prices while vendors save on middleman costs. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

Secure & Reliable

Bank-grade security for all transactions. Verified profiles, quality checks, and escrow payment protection ensure safe trading for everyone.

Integrated Logistics

Partner logistics network ensures efficient delivery. Track shipments in real-time and coordinate pickups seamlessly through the platform.

Data-Driven Insights

Access market analytics, demand forecasts, and pricing trends. Make informed decisions backed by real agricultural market data.
